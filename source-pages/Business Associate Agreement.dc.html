A contract that makes us legally accountable for PHI.

A Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is a HIPAA-required contract between a healthcare provider (the “Covered Entity”) and a service partner that handles Protected Health Information on its behalf (the “Business Associate”). It defines how PHI may be used, how it must be protected, and who is liable if something goes wrong.

When Carentix manages your scheduling, prior authorizations, verification, scribing, or records, we are your Business Associate. The BAA is what turns our security promises into binding, enforceable obligations.

Protected Health Information (PHI)

Any individually identifiable health information — names, contact details, insurance and claims data, appointment and clinical records — that we encounter while supporting your practice. We use only the minimum necessary, always.

Required by law. Signed before day one.
